About Me

I’m a FrontEnd Developer driven by curiosity, clarity, and the desire to build things that actually make sense.

My journey into tech started with wanting to understand how systems work and how ideas turn into usable products. Over time, I found myself drawn to front-end development because it’s where logic, structure, and human experience meet.

Beyond building, I’ve taken on leadership roles that shaped how I approach problem-solving and collaboration. I’m a pioneer and director of Genesys Tech Club, FUTO, where I’ve helped build a community around learning, growth, and practical tech exposure. Leading teams and initiatives taught me how to think beyond code—about people, processes, and delivery.

I value clean thinking, intentional design, and solutions that scale. I enjoy breaking down complex problems, asking better questions, and refining ideas until they’re clear and usable. Progress matters more to me than perfection, and I believe growth comes from building, reflecting, and improving continuously.

This portfolio represents my work, my leadership, and my ongoing journey as a developer. I'm always open to discussing new projects, creative ideas, or opportunities to be part of your vision. Let's build something amazing together.
